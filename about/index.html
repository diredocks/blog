Site / 池塘

In fact, the ponds I’ve encountered growing up were all dirty, but that doesn’t stop me from imagining an ideal pond—or better yet, a lake.

My good friends and I are on a small boat, chatting, with nothing to worry about. Dew drops rest on the lotus leaves, it’s that blue morning hue, misty. Nearby is a forest, a stream, a flat forest, with light rain drizzling on us.

The whole world stands still. Oh my God!

We drift slowly to the center of the pond. Everything is so harmonious, natural, and beautiful.

其实长这么大接触到的池塘都是脏脏的，不过这并不妨碍我幻想一个美好的池塘——甚至最好不是池塘，是湖泊。

我和我的好朋友们，在一艘小船上，聊天，啥都不用想。池塘的荷叶上有露珠，早上那种蓝色的，雾蒙蒙的，旁边是森林，小溪，平整的森林，淅沥沥的小雨飘在我们身上。

整个世界都静止了，天啊。

我们慢慢飘到池塘中心，一切都是这么和谐、自然、美好。
